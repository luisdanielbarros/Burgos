About Us

Our restaurant is a family business located in Lisbon with 32 years of specialization in cooking hamburgers.

We mix our portuguese tradition with mostly american fast food cuisine, even though we try to take a little bit from everywhere around the world and give it on our spin.

Because that's what being a cook means, we are like small scientists.
